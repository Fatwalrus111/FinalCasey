Signing up

Camp Casey prides itself on being free for everyone of all needs. To sign up please go to our contact page and reach out. We maintain a 1-1 camper counsler ratio and we typically have 60 spots available. If you have any questions or concerns let us know. Casey is a camp for people with primarily physical disabilities but we support people with secondary mental disabilities. We are not designed for people with exclusively mental disabilities and if during the week your child proves to be to disruptive we will need to send them home.

Our Activities

Campers will enjoy a suite of activities, from Waterpalooza to the Casey Dance and Carnival we strive to provide everyone with a camp opportunity that parallels that of regular summer camps. We have an incredibly supportive camp community with comppassionate volunteers and counslers who have been thouroughly vetted and background checked

  • Go Karts
  • Costume Parade
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Dancing
  • Accesible Sports
  • Talent Show

Accomodations

At Camp Casey we strive to accomodate all campers. Our camp barracks are wheel chair and power chair accessible. All bathrooms and other camper facilities are also fully camper accessible. Our kitchen is able to meet any dietry restrictions and needs, if your child struggles to eat by themselves or is fully dependent we are capable of assisting. Counslers will always be on duty within the barracks including during the night. All counslers are trained in Bullying and Sexaul Assault prevention. We have a highly qualified nursing staff on 24 hour stand by in the event of an emergency.
