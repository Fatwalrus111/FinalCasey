Building Confidence, Creating Friendships

Kiwanis Camp Casey is a free week-long camp for over 80 youth aged 6-17 with physical disabilities. Join us to experience growth, joy, and community.

Our Mission

At Camp Casey, we aim to empower children with disabilities, helping them embrace their individuality and create lasting friendships.

Get Involved

Volunteer, donate, or simply spread the word. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of these amazing children.
