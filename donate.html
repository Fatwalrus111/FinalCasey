How Your Donations Help

Your contributions provide food, lodging, and activities for campers. Every dollar goes toward creating a magical experience for the kids. We are 100% community funded and every donation will allow us to expand activities both in camp and outside. Because of continued generosity we have been able to set up a scholarship to help aid some of our former campers who are hoping to attend college
