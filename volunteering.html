Why Volunteer?

Volunteering at Camp Casey is an unforgettable experience that will leave a lasting impression. Help make camp the amazing place it is and give these children an unforgettable experiance. For many of these families, caring for a loved one is also a full time job, help give them an opportunity to have rest.

“Working with the other volunteers is the most rewarding and satisfying work I have ever done. The laughter and smiles on the faces of the children make it all worthwhile.” – Dick Murrary

How to Sign Up

Fill out our contact form or email us at volunteer@campcasey.org. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and skill levels!
